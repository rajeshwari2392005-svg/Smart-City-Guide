Top Tourist Attractions in Bangalore

Discover the most popular places to visit in Bangalore.

Bangalore Palace

A magnificent royal palace known for its Tudor-style architecture, beautiful gardens and rich history.

Lalbagh Botanical Garden

A famous botanical garden with rare plants, a beautiful glass house and peaceful walking paths.

Cubbon Park

A large green park in the heart of Bangalore, perfect for walking, jogging and relaxation.

ISKCON Temple

A beautiful temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, known for its peaceful atmosphere and stunning architecture.

Bannerghatta National Park

A popular wildlife park featuring a safari, zoo, butterfly park and nature trails.

Vidhana Soudha

The iconic seat of the Karnataka State Legislature, famous for its grand architecture and historical importance.
